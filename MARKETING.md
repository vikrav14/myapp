- Send a **receipt or Juice screenshot** → merchant, amount, category logged
- Brain dump: *"I spent 180 on mine frite"* → extracted automatically
- **`my runway`** — days till payday, daily pace, breathing room in Rs
- **`payday 25`** · **`salary 25000`**

#### Reminders

`remind me to call mum at 6pm`

`calendar add team sync on friday at 3pm`
